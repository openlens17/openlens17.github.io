About the App

We created this app as our submission for the 2017 code4pa hackathon. As residents of the city of Harrisburg we wanted to create a way to boost civil engagement and tourism throughout the city.

Our team

We are a team of college students at Harrisburg University. We build applications leveraging emerging technologies in the field of computer science.
